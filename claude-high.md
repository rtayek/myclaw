### **MyClaw: High-Level Vision Statement (Updated)**

> 
> To provide a zero-friction, 24/7 persistent AI assistant that combines local-first system control and privacy with cloud persistence, accessible directly through daily messaging platforms.
> 
> 
> MyClaw bridges the gap between simple messaging interfaces and advanced multi-agent execution. By serving as a lightweight orchestration layer , MyClaw delivers power without complexity—combining **local file and system access** , **messaging-first interaction** , **dynamic skill loading** , **intelligent hybrid model routing** , **self-hosted micro-app generation** , and **persistent background memory** into a single, set-and-forget runtime.
>
