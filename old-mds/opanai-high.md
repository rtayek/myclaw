## High-Level Vision

**MyClaw is an accessible, local-first AI cockpit that lets people work with many interchangeable AI systems while preserving their conversations, context, and artifacts in durable, user-owned records.**

It should combine:

* accessible desktop interaction through large text, keyboard, voice, touch, and audio;
* a provider-independent harness for local models, cloud models, and specialized tools;
* persistent sessions, searchable history, skills, memory, and context handoffs;
* a durable backend that captures events before presenting them and keeps originals separate from derived summaries;
* optional supervised or unattended agent loops under explicit user policy.

The first priority remains a reliable daily-use desktop client. More advanced routing, automation, and agent behavior should grow from that foundation without weakening accessibility, transparency, or ownership.
